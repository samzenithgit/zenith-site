Case Studies

Selected projects from our team's portfolio of 100+ economic impact studies.

The studies below are a small selection from more than 100 economic impact analyses our team has authored for public agencies, universities, festivals, ports, pension systems, and private sector clients. Each was conducted under the same methodological standard: an input-output model calibrated to the study geography, conservative treatment of net new versus substitutional spending, transparent assumptions documented in a methodology appendix, and deliverables designed for the specific audience that would read them — whether that is a state legislature, a municipal board, a sponsorship committee, or the national press.

We chose these case studies because they illustrate the range of our work. Some are national in scope (the U.S. Data Center Construction report). Some cover every county in a state (Montana Statewide Aviation). Some map impacts to individual legislative districts (LACERA). Some are multi-tenant campus studies (Port San Antonio). Each one used different tools, different data sources, and different deliverable formats — but each was built on the same principle that the findings should hold up to scrutiny from anyone who reads the methodology section.

Click through any of the studies below for the full write-up, including methodology, key findings, and links to the published report or press coverage where available.
