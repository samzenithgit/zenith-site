Research

Publications

Thought leadership and research from Zenith Economics.

Zenith Economics publishes original research on topics where rigorous economic analysis is in short supply. Our publications are independent, methodologically transparent, and produced to the same standard as the client engagements that fund the firm. We do not accept sponsored research, and we do not publish work where we have a financial stake in the outcome.

Each report is modeled in IMPLAN (or, where appropriate, REMI or RIMS II) with regional multipliers calibrated to the study geography, and every assumption is documented in a methodology appendix. Our flagship U.S. Data Center Construction report — which found that the sector supports 571,000+ U.S. jobs annually — is produced to the same standard.

Our work has been cited by Billboard, NBC Montana, Texas Public Radio, the San Antonio Report, the Joplin Globe, Urbanize LA, and in official reporting by the Texas Comptroller of Public Accounts. We take press citations as a signal that the underlying methodology is sound — journalists and regulators do not typically stake their credibility on shaky numbers.

New reports are released on an opportunistic basis, typically when a client engagement surfaces a broader research question that deserves a standalone treatment. If you have a research question you think Zenith should study, tell us about it.
