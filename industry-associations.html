Industry Association Economic Impact Studies

How We Help

Industry associations and trade groups exist to advocate for their members, and few tools are more powerful for advocacy than an independent economic impact study. Whether you represent manufacturers, healthcare providers, agricultural producers, or technology companies, quantifying your sector's contribution to jobs, GDP, and tax revenue gives your organization credible data for legislative testimony, media outreach, and member engagement.

We design studies that aggregate member-level data into sector-wide findings, with breakdowns by state, region, or legislative district as needed. Our reports are designed for public audiences: clear, visually polished, and built to be cited by journalists and policymakers.

Methodology

What We Measure

Sector Employment

Total direct jobs across all member organizations, with industry and occupational breakdowns.

Economic Output

Aggregate revenue, sales, and production value across the represented industry.

Supply Chain Effects

Indirect spending by member businesses on goods and services from other sectors.

Tax Revenue

Combined fiscal contributions across all levels of government.

Wage & Income Analysis

Average compensation, benefits, and total labor income supported by the sector.

Geographic Distribution

Impact mapped by state, metro area, or legislative district for targeted advocacy.
