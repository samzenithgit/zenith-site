Real Estate & EB-5

Real Estate & EB-5 Economic Impact Studies

How We Help

Real estate development creates significant economic activity during both construction and long-term operations. For developers seeking EB-5 financing, demonstrating job creation that meets USCIS methodological requirements is essential to project viability. For market-rate and affordable housing projects, economic impact data supports zoning applications, community engagement, and public subsidy requests.

We produce EB-5 compliant job creation analyses, development feasibility studies, highest-and-best-use analyses, and general economic impact studies for residential, commercial, industrial, and mixed-use projects of all sizes.
