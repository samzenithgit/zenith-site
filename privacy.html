Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who We Are

Zenith Economics, LLC is an independent economic consulting firm based in Los Angeles, California. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information gathered through our website at zenithecon.com.

What We Collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect the information you provide, which may include your name, email address, phone number, organization name, and project description. This information is transmitted to us via FormSubmit.co, a third-party form processing service.

We do not collect information automatically. Our website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics services, or any other passive data collection technologies. We do not use Google Analytics or any similar service.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry, prepare a proposal if requested, and communicate with you about potential or active engagements. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by FormSubmit.co, which receives and forwards form submissions to our email. FormSubmit.co's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

Our website is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal web hosting operations. We do not access or use these logs for tracking or marketing purposes.

Survey Sweepstakes

From time to time, we conduct survey sweepstakes in connection with our economic impact studies. Information collected through these surveys is used for research purposes and sweepstakes administration as described in the specific terms and conditions for each sweepstakes. Survey data is used in aggregate form in our research and individual responses are not published.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and related correspondence for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which they were collected, typically for the duration of a potential or active client relationship. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we have collected, the right to request deletion of your information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
